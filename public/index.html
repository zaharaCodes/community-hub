The Problem

Help exists. Finding it is the hard part.

Millions of people struggle not because resources don't exist — but because no one told them where to look.

01

Resources are scattered

Food banks, clinics, shelters — they're spread across dozens of websites, Facebook groups, and paper flyers that no one sees.

02

Information goes stale

Hours change. Programs end. Phone numbers disconnect. Most resource directories haven't been updated in years.

03

Word of mouth doesn't scale

The best tip is usually from a friend who's been there. Community Hub makes that tip available to everyone.

04

No one owns the problem

Government sites are bureaucratic. Nonprofits are underfunded. A community-powered platform can move faster.

How It Works

Simple by design. Powerful by community.

Three steps. No account required to browse.

01

Search & Discover

Browse by category, location, or keyword. Filter to exactly what you need. No account required — help is immediately accessible.

02

Save & Share

Bookmark resources for later. Copy a direct link to share with someone who needs it. Vote helpful to surface the best resources.

03

Contribute Back

Create an account to share resources you've used. Add tags, contact info, and descriptions. Edit or update anytime.
