Our commitment: Community Hub does not sell your data. We do not run ads. We collect only what's necessary to run the platform. Your privacy is a feature, not an afterthought.

01

What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the platform. Here's exactly what we store:

Data TypeWhat We StoreWhy
Account infoName, email address, hashed passwordTo create and authenticate your account
ProfileOptional bio, avatar color, initialsTo personalize your profile
ResourcesName, description, location, tags, contact info you submitTo display community resources
ActivityHelpful votes you cast, resources you submittedTo show your dashboard stats and prevent duplicate votes
TimestampsAccount creation date, resource submission datesTo show recency of resources

Browsing Community Hub without an account is completely anonymous. We do not require an account to view resources.

02

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect only for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating your login and keeping your session secure
  • Displaying your submitted resources and dashboard statistics
  • Preventing spam and duplicate helpful votes
  • Sending transactional emails (e.g. password reset) — never marketing emails without consent
  • Improving platform performance and fixing bugs

We will never use your data to serve advertisements, sell to third parties, build behavioral profiles, or contact you with unsolicited marketing.

03

Data We Don't Collect

To be explicit about what we do not collect:

  • Your precise location or GPS coordinates
  • Browsing history or behavior tracking
  • Device fingerprints or persistent identifiers
  • Payment information of any kind — the platform is free
  • Phone numbers (unless you voluntarily add them to a resource listing)
  • Social media profiles or connections
04

Cookies & Local Storage

Community Hub uses minimal browser storage to provide core functionality:

Storage TypeWhat's StoredPurpose
localStorageAuth token (JWT), your bookmarks, your vote historyKeep you logged in, save bookmarks locally
Session cookiesBasic session stateSecurity and authentication

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or analytics platforms like Google Analytics.

You can clear your localStorage at any time through your browser settings. This will sign you out and clear saved bookmarks on that device.

05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Period.

We may share limited data only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service providers: Our hosting provider and email provider process data on our behalf under strict data agreements
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users
  • Business transfer: If Community Hub is acquired or merged, users will be notified before data is transferred

Google Fonts is loaded from Google's servers to render our typography. This means Google's servers process your IP address when fonts load.

06

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12 — never stored in plain text
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with expiry — not persistent cookies
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Our database is not publicly accessible

However, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to our contact email rather than publicly disclosing it.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

Access
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
Correction
Update or correct your name, email, and profile information
Deletion
Delete your account and associated personal data at any time
Portability
Request an export of your submitted resources and account data
Objection
Object to certain uses of your data — we'll review and respond
Restriction
Request we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your name, email, and password are permanently deleted immediately
  • Resources you submitted may remain as anonymous community contributions unless you request removal
  • Helpful votes attributed to your account are anonymized
  • Backups may retain data for up to 30 days before permanent deletion

To request complete deletion of all your data including submitted resources, contact us at the address in Section 10.

09

Children's Privacy

Community Hub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information promptly.

10

Contact & Data Requests

For any privacy-related questions, data access requests, or deletion requests:

  • Email: privacy@communityhub.app
  • Subject line: Include "Privacy Request" for faster processing
  • Response time: Within 30 days for all requests

You are always in control. You can update your profile, change your password, or delete your account entirely from your dashboard settings at any time — no need to contact us.
